Reading Groups

Inspired after MIT's URGE program, the ACSU has initiated our own undergraduate Reading Groups.
Our reading group are open for anyone who is interested, regardless of background.

Overview

ACSU Reading groups are small groups of less than 10 undergraduate students meeting regularly to discuss technical papers to encourage learning in a casual environment. These groups would be open for anyone who is interested, regardless of background. Each reading group would have a reading group leader who would be responsible for choosing the technical paper and leading the discussions. The participants would read the paper beforehand, come prepared with 1-2 questions and be ready to join the discussion.
