Mailing List

Join our mailing list to get notified about relevant opportunities (e.g. internships/jobs), our upcoming events, and other events of interest in the Cornell Engineering community. Anyone is welcome to join at no cost. Just fill in your email below to subscribe!






Officer Applications

Would you like to get involved with helping ACSU? We are looking for candidates who are responsible, hardworking, and always taking initiative. Our work spans across a number of different teams. As an officer, not only will you have a chance to give back to the CS community, you will meet and work with the amazing people in our officer team! Applications are open until March 4th, 2019.



Membership

Paid ACSU membership is open to all Cornell undergraduate students. Members receive a t-shirt, are invited to submit their resumes to our resume book, and enjoy many other benefits. To join ACSU, attend and sign in at least one general meeting and pay the annual dues of only $5. Membership is not required to subscribe to our mailing list.



Note that ACSU membership lasts for an academic school year, regardless of which semester you paid. For instance, if you pay your dues anytime in Fall 2018 or Spring 2019, you will be an ACSU paid member until the end of the Spring 2019 semester.
